Phase 4 — The "How Would YC See Your Idea" Feature 💡
Time: 3 days
Goal: Your killer feature — user describes startup, gets a YC-style assessment

Step 1 — Build the input form
User fills in:

One sentence description of their startup
Industry
Target customer — B2B or B2C
Current stage — idea, prototype, live, revenue
Team size and background

Step 2 — Find similar YC companies
Take their description → embed it → search your company collection for the 5 most semantically similar YC companies → return them with their batch, status, and description
Step 3 — Retrieve relevant YC wisdom
Use their industry + stage to pull relevant chunks from PG essays and YC blog posts about that space
Step 4 — Generate the assessment
Send to Claude with a specific prompt structured as a YC application review:

What's genuinely interesting about this idea
What a YC partner would push back on
Which similar YC companies succeeded and why
One specific question a YC interviewer would ask them
Honest assessment of fit

Step 5 — Add a disclaimer
Every assessment ends with — "This is based on patterns from public YC data. Real YC decisions depend on many factors including team, timing, and market conditions."
✅ Phase 4 done when the feature gives genuinely useful, specific feedback that references real YC companies and thinking.
