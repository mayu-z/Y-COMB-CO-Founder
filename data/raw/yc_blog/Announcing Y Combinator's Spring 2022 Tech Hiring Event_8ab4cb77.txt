Announcing Y Combinator's Spring 2022 Tech Hiring Event
February 28, 2023·by Ryan Choi
We're excited to host an in-person tech hiring event on Wednesday, March 8th at 5:30 PM at Y Combinator's San Francisco office. This will be the first in-person hiring event we're hosting at YC in over two years, and we have 9 YC-backed startups who are looking to hire engineers and technical talent.
The event will feature brief founder & company overviews, time to network, and light food and drinks provided by YC. Spots are limited, so apply now to get on the list.
The companies attending (and what they're hiring for) below:
OneSignal (Growth): Engage customers through personalized omni-channel messaging (Infrastructure and Product Engineers, Product Managers)
Memora Health (Series A): Helps healthcare organizations digitize and automate complex care workflows
Seam (Series A): API for IoT Devices (Engineering & Sales)
Slope (Series A): Buy Now, Pay Later for B2B (Backend & Full stack engineers; data; customer success)
Fieldguide (Series A): Automation and collaboration software for audit and advisory firms (ML Engineer, Product Manager)
Agave (Seed): Plaid for Construction (Founding Engineer in PHP/Typescript/React)
Mintlify (Seed): Developer-first documentation that converts users (Full Stack Engineer)
Replo (Seed): Visual development for React, starting with e-commerce (Engineers & Designers)
OneShop (Seed): Operating system for reselling online (Backend, Frontend and Growth Engineers)
If you're an engineer interested in working at a fast-growing startup, this is an opportunity to meet founders and hiring managers in person to learn about their companies and products, understand their technology stacks and challenges, and get a sense of their culture and what it's like to work there.
This is a unique chance to engage with multiple companies at once, and to potentially find your next role at a leading tech startup. We hope you'll join us for what will be an informative and worthwhile evening.
