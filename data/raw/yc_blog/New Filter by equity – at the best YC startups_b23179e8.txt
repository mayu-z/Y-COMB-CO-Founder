New: Filter by equity – at the best YC startups
August 3, 2022·by Ryan Choi
YC's job board now lets job seekers filter by equity offered on the job description. Over 380 startups have posted roles with equity, with some early engineering roles offering 2%.
At YC, we tell our founders to be generous with equity, and even suggest giving 10% to the first 10 employees. Early employees are taking a bigger risk by working at an early stage startup, and their upside should be significantly greater than employees at later stage or public companies. 
Below are a few startups on the platform hiring and providing equity compensation on their job listing. See the full list of startups by creating a profile on YC's job board and find yours.
