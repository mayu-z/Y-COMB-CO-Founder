YC's Spring Tour 2022 | Y Combinator
January 28, 2022·by Tamanna Khemani
We are excited to announce our 2022 Spring Outreach Tour. Our goal is to virtually meet founders and future founders from cities and universities around the world. At each location, we will host talks with YC group partners and YC alumni, who will discuss their founder journey, the YC experience and myths around starting startups.
We will also be hosting two virtual admissions sessions to chat about the YC application process, give application tips and answer your questions about the batch program.
This year, we’re working with AWS Activate to provide $1,000 in AWS credits to spend on AWS services, and $350 in Developer Support plan credits to attendees. This is on top of any AWS credits you may have received from AWS Activate previously. To learn more about AWS Activate, please visit www.aws.com/activate.
Interested in applying to YC? Applications for YC Summer 2022 are now open.
